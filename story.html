Edgar the Baby Elephant

Today, we're going on an exciting adventure into the world of words. We'll learn about special letter combinations and word structures that make you feel like true word detectives.

First, let's talk about three-letter consonants. These are groups of three consonants that work together to make a unique sound.

For example, the word 'scratch' has the three-letter consonant 'tch' at the end, which makes a clear 'tch' sound.
